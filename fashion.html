Slippio - Stay Simple With Style

Slippio has been introducing simple and relax in the fashion world with slippers. Eventhough it heards funny, but it's a unique ideas by changing the perspective and mindset of slippers is a wearings that we wear when we feel lazy. With a correct attire and nice color, slippers can turn into something fashionable but simple. In addition, it seems affordable by most of the people to stay in current trending.

Let's lean in a little closer, and you see that these slippers are not anti-fashion, but nu-fashion. All the major shoe trends of the past decade have been filtered into footwear you can wear when your outfit is accessorised by the telly remote rather than an evening bag. Furry shoes? They’ve been around since Phoebe Philo put fluff-lined pool sliders on the Celine catwalk in 2014, darling. Backless silhouettes? Some of us started wearing them in 2015 when Alessandro Michele did backless loafers in his first Gucci collection. JW Anderson’s cult padded crossover slides, a long-time front-row favourite, gave us the cartoonish proportions now found in flouncy high-street slippers. And what are North Face Thermoballs, if not puffer jackets for your feet? Wear your slippers with pride. Right now, they are as fashion forward as feet can get.
